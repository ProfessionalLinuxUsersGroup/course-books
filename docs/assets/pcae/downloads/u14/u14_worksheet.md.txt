### Unit 14 Discussion Post 2

Your team has a wide variety of open source tooling that they
use for monitoring. The structure and web of technologies has gotten too large to maintain
and documentation for it is not able to help the junior engineers keep things running
without tribal knowledge. You want to find a viable set of monitoring tools that are well
supported and work together. You have found yourself here: https://landscape.cncf.io/

1. What are some viable observability tools on that page?
2. What considerations would you need to think about bringing them into your test environment?
